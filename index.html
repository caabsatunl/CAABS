About

The Commutative Algebra and Adjacent Book-club (CAAB) is an opprotunity for graduate students and post docs at UNL to read, discuss, and learn about topics in commutative algebra or adjacent fields. In contrary to CAS and CARS , this book club is not a seminar. In particular, there will be not talks or lectures in the traditional sense. Instead, attendees will discuss weekly or biweekly, in book club fashion, selected sections of a text on a predetermined topic.

Goals

  • Learn about commutative algebra and related areas
  • Foster a sense of community within the commutative algebra and adjacent research area
  • Practice respectful and productive discourse about mathematics.
